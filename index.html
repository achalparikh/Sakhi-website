Beauty School

To spread the knowledge



We develop in our students the highest possible degree of professional, social and technical competence.

We teach the principles and practices of cosmetology and esthetics from scratch all the way up to the most advanced techniques used in the industry.

The curriculum is delivered with a combination of theory lectures and hands-on practice using mannequins and actual clientele.

About us

Our story from the beginning.

  • 2001

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In year 2001, Our founder Parul Parikh started this business in a small apartment in Avishkar complex

  • 2005

    Sakhi getting up on it's feet

    In a few short years Sakhi started a climb towards becoming a successful business by providing the best in class services.

  • 2007

    International Endavours

    In 2007, Mrs. Parul Parikh moved to Toronto, Canada. Over the span of one year she learned many new trends and techniques in the area of make-up, hair style, and asthetics.

  • 2008

    Keeping up with the Latest Trends

    After coming back from Canada, Sakhi was introduced to new found knowledge about market trends and techniques through our founder.

  • 2010

    Journey to the top

    After a decade, Sakhi is considered the one of the best make-up studios in the city. We made sure that every customer was looked after with top-notch quality services and latest products.

  • 2015

    On top of the World

    In 2015, Parul Parikh claimed one of the most prestigeous title in the country. She broke the Limca book of records for maximum bridal make-ups in one day.

Our Founder

Parul Parikh

Mrs. Parul Parikh graduated from M.S University of Vadodara with a Bachelors in chemistry. She has been involved in social and philanthropist work with organizations such as Stri Niketan, Jaycees and the Lions club. “I believe that age should not be contribute to how beautiful a woman can look!”

After learning from the best in the industry including training in multiple programs in Canada, she provides her clients with the latest and greatest services in the beauty industry.
